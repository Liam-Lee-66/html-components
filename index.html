    • Blasters During the Clone Wars

    This website will be explaining about Blaster rifles used between the Galactic Republic and the Separatists.

    The reason is simple, there are too many types of models from different planets. Also, both the Galactic Republic and the Separatists are organized to use specific Blasters throughout the war.


    The Galactic Republic

    DC 15A

    The DC 15A was commonly used throughout the Clone Wars. It was first used starting from the First Battle of Geonosis. This specific model can be seen several times in "Star Wars: Attack of the Clones (Episode 2)"


    DC 15S

    The DC 15S was also a commonly used Blaster throughout the Clone Wars.

    The main difference between the DC 15A, and the DC 15S were:

  • Smaller in size
  • Higher rate of fire
  • Lower firing range

    • It's smaller size and higher rate of fire was much useful in close-quater combats.

    This model can be seen several times in "Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith (Episode 3)"
